Registration

All members of FF'18 will have access to movies at all locations. MEMBERSHIP ID'S ARE REQUIRED ON ENTRY. Non-members can purshase tickets or collect their pre booked tickets on location. Pre booking options are available online or through the app.

Cost
  • General Delegate: $200 (Festival Catalogue & Delegate Bag inc.)
  • Student Delegate: $170
  • Film Club Members: $150 (Festival Catalogue & Delegate Bag inc.)
  • Film Society Members: $170

Attendance Info

NO REFUNDS will be issued once tickets have been purchased. Showtimings are subject to change in case of unforseen circumstances. In such a case, new tickets will be issued free of charge with respect to the cancelled show.

Delegate passes once purchased cannot be returned but exchange of passes can be done at select theaters. In such a case, both parties are required to be present with necessary identification.

New passes can be issued on lost or theft with proper identification.

Furthur queries can be answered through our helpline number or email us at attendancequeries@filmfestival18.com
